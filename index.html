About me

I am an Associate Professor at the College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering at Hunan University. I was a Research Assistant Professor at the Southern University of Science and Technology from June 2020 to April 2022. I earned my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science at Wayne State University in 2020. Before starting my Ph.D., I had 4 years of experience in the industry. I completed my Master's degree and Bachelor's degree at Tongji University in 2011 and 2008, respectively.

Research

My primary research interests are in different areas of security and privacy, including system security, mobile security, IoT security, transportation security, trusted execution environment, and hardware-assisted security.
I am always looking for self-motivated master students and postdocs who are interested in system security and determined to publish high-quality research papers. Please feel free to shoot me an email and let's enjoy research together.
